About State Bank of India

India's largest and most trusted financial institution with a legacy of excellence spanning over two centuries.

Our Heritage

State Bank of India (SBI) is an Indian multinational public sector bank and financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. SBI is the largest bank in India by assets and has a proud history dating back to 1806 when the Bank of Calcutta, later renamed the Bank of Bengal, was established.

Today, SBI serves over 450 million customers through its vast network of branches and ATMs across India and maintains a significant international presence in 36 countries.
